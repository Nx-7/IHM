How To Do It

4 Steps

01

Stand Directly Below a Pull-Up Bar

Place your hands in an overhand grip (palms facing away from your body) with your hands slightly further than shoulder-width apart.

02

Lift Your Feet Up

Lift your feet up so that you're hanging from the bar, and engage your core by pulling your belly button in toward your spine. Pull your shoulders back and down.

03

Pull Yourself Up Towards The Bar

Engaging the muscles in your arms and back, bend your elbows and raise your upper body up toward the bar until your chin is over the bar.

04

Slowly Lower Yourself

At the top of the movement, inhale. Then extend your elbows and lower your body back down to the starting position.
